GlobaLID continues to grow

Thanks to the efforts of our regional editors, the core team, and our contributors, the amount of data in the GlobaLID database continued to grow significantly from 4147 data points at the end of 2023 to 5589 data points. Beside major revisions and significant additions to already covered data, GlobaLID now includes data from the Alps and Central America. You can find the full list of countries for which data were added on the News page of the web application.

More data is currently reviewed and prepared – stay tuned!
